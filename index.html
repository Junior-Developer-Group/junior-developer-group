Junior Developer Group

We're a small (and very friendly) international non-profit group that serves a community of early career developers entering their first roles in tech with workshops, group and personal projects, skill-appropriate code reviews, weekly catchups, 1-1 guidance, exposure to Agile/Scrum terminology and usage of tools such as Jira and GitHub while working on group projects.
